Our Experience

We come from the world of social sciences and are trained in the teaching of Spanish from a perspective that links culture with languages.

Socio-cultural approach

From the culture, arts, literature , tourism and gastronomy we will try to make you learn, grow and communicate.

How do we do it?

USEFUL TOOLS

We facilitate the language

We create tools so you can incorporate Spanish into your daily life through culture. We are eternal learners; we understand your needs as we never stop learning.

Pragmatic approach

We have a critical spirit that joined us to create a methodology based on practical and modern approaches. We want to let you create your own method in a creative and disruptive way.

ABOUT OUR LESSONS

Duration and course

We offer two weekly meetings of one hour or one and a half hours; online or in person in Buenos Aires and Florence. We use Google Classroom and Google Meet.

Teaching materials

Personal Google presentation, extra exercises and material adapted to your goals and interests: grammar worksheets, interactive games, series proposals, podcasts, videos.

INDIVIDUAL CLASSES

  • Are you looking for an extra-personal, communicative, disruptive and dynamic approach?
  • Do you enjoy learning languages through cultural exchange?
  • Are you looking to improve conversation, improve comprehension, work on phonetics and increase your Spanish vocabulary?
  • Are you looking for modern approaches to understanding grammar in the context in which native speakers use it?

GROUP LESSONS

  • You are looking for a personalized, dynamic and disruptive service, but you want to share it with a group of people in the same situation as you.
  • You want to meet nice people from all over the world and share travel experiences in Italy and Argentina.
  • You like to learn from others, do group activities in class and exchange in class.

WORKSHOPS

  • Individual meetings where you will work on an idea or project that the student brings in their native language.
  • We are prepared to teach Spanish in the fields of political science, journalism and international relations.
  • Your goal is to communicate fluently in Spanish, with a rich variety of content applicable to the business world.
